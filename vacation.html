    1.DRAWBRIDGE

    Towering over the Terengganu River, the Kuala Terengganu Drawbridge stands as a majestic symbol of progress and heritage. This awe-inspiring structure, inaugurated in 2019, is not just a vital transportation link but also a tourist attraction and a source of local pride. Near to Drawbridge they held a lot of activities such as light festival, food truck and food festival. The tourist also can take a walk on the skybridge, a glass-floored walkway offering breathtaking 360-degree views of the city, the river, and the surrounding landscape. It's a perfect spot for capturing stunning photos and memories.

    2.PANTAI MIAMI

    Nestled amidst the lush greenery of Seberang Takir, Kuala Terengganu, lies a hidden gem of Pantai Miami. Unlike its namesake, this beach exudes a tranquil charm, far removed from the glitz and glamour of its American counterpart. Here, swaying palm trees whisper secrets to the gentle breeze, and the turquoise waters beckon with the promise of serenity. Pantai Miami unfolds like a scene from a tropical dream. Pristine white sand stretches as far as the eye can see, offering a soft cushion for sunbathers and sandcastle builders alike. The crystal-clear waters are perfect for a refreshing dip, while gentle waves lap against the shore, creating a soothing rhythm. Lush vegetation fringes the beach, providing shade and a habitat for diverse birdlife. The air hums with the melody of nature, creating a symphony that calms the soul.

    3.PANTAI TELUK KETAPANG

    Pantai Teluk Kepang is relaxed sandy beach with many vendors for the local batter-fried fish specialty "ikan celup tepung". One of the most magical experiences at Pantai Teluk Ketapang is witnessing the breathtaking sunset over the South China Sea. As the sun dips below the horizon, the sky transforms into a canvas of warm hues, casting a golden glow over the entire beach. The tranquil atmosphere and the play of colors make this a perfect moment for couples, families, or solo travelers seeking a moment of reflection and awe. Not only that there are a lot of activities that people can do here. One of the activities is camping.
