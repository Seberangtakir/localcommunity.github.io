    1.ANJUNG PANTAI SEBERANG TAKIR

    Anjung Pantai is one of an immediately transported to a world of serenity. Rows of charming chalets, built in traditional Malay architecture, stand amidst lush greenery, their wooden exteriors blending harmoniously with the natural surroundings. The air hums with the gentle melody of the ocean, a constant invitation to relax and unwind. Each chalet offers a haven of comfort, equipped with essential amenities to ensure a pleasant stay. Verandas, adorned with comfortable seating, beckon guests to step outside and embrace the breathtaking sea views.

    2.MASJID SEBERANG TAKIR

    The architectural style of Masjid Seberang Takir is a captivating blend of traditional Malay elements and modern influences. The main prayer hall, adorned with intricate calligraphy and geometric patterns, evokes a sense of awe and serenity. The towering minaret, reaching towards the heavens, serves as a beacon of faith and guidance. Surrounding the mosque are lush gardens, providing a tranquil space for reflection and prayer. Funded entirely through public donations, the mosque embodies the spirit of unity and collaboration that binds the people of Seberang Takir together.
