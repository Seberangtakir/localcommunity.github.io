    • SHOPPING MALL NEAR TO KG SEBERANG TAKIR

    1.KUALA TERENGGANU CITY CENTER (KTCC)

    KTCC Mall is indeed the first and largest modern shopping mall in Kuala Terengganu, gracing the city with its presence since its grand opening in January 2020. It's not only a shopping haven but also a landmark and a popular spot for locals and tourists alike. The mall's design further elevates the experience. Natural light streams in through strategically placed skylights, bathing the interior in a warm glow. Lush greenery accents the corridors, adding a touch of nature's serenity amidst the urban symphony. Comfortable seating areas provide respite for weary shoppers, while art installations and cultural displays offer glimpses into the rich heritage of Terengganu.

    2.MAYANG MALL

    Mayang Mall is a new lifestyle mall that will soon blossom in Kuala Terengganu that anchored by SOGO, Kuala Lumpur's largest department store and bring the best of Japan to Kuala Terengganu. Scheduled to unveil its doors around Raya 2024, Mayang Mall occupies a strategic location just a stone's throw from the iconic Pasar Payang market. This convenient accessibility, coupled with its proximity to the Drawbridge, ensures it will draw not only local patrons but also shoppers from neighboring states like Kelantan and Pahang.
